Mads Gilbert is a Norwegian emergency medicine specialist who has made frequent trips to Palestinian refugee camps in Lebanon, the West Bank and Gaza since 1982.

The Israeli government has permanently banned him from entering the Gaza Strip.“They forbid us to be there with them… but they can’t forbid us to help them!”

He has donated more than
200,000 kr.
